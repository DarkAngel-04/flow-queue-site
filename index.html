Flow Queue

Queue prompts, automate image and video runs, and organize downloads for Google Flow™.

Built for repeatable creative work

  • Run multiple image or video prompts from a Chrome side panel.
  • Control concurrency, delays, retries, models, aspect ratios, and output count.
  • Assign reference images and monitor every prompt's status.
  • Automatically download and rename completed media.
  • Keep settings and diagnostics locally in your browser.
